Our Story

A Taste of Home,
Since 1993

Nestled in the heart of Dhading Bensi's Milantole, Paju Lounge & Family Restaurant has been a gathering place for families, friends, and food lovers for over three decades.

What started as a simple family restaurant has grown into Dhading's most cherished dining experience — a place where rustic warmth meets modern comfort. Our kitchen brings you freshly prepared dishes made with care, served in an ambience that makes every visit feel like coming home.

Whether you're here for a quiet family dinner, a casual hangout with friends, or to unwind to live music on the weekend — Paju Lounge is your place.
